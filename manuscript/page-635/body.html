“The Almighty made,  
Down the Hebron vale,  
With his plastic hands,  
   Adam’s fair form:

And five hundred years,  
Void of any help,  
There he remained and lay  
   Without a soul.

He again did form,  
In calm paradise,  
From a left-side rib,  
   Bliss-throbbing Eve.
