Of man-woman.

And once, not hidden,  
She brought forth Abel,  
And Cain the forlorn,  
   The homicide.

To him and his mate  
Was given a spade,  
To break up the soil,  
   Thus to get bread.

The wheat pure and white,  
Summer tilth to sow,  
Every man to feed,  
   Till great yule feast.

An angelic hand
